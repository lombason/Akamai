Change Safety Deployment is responsible for the maintenance and execution of the steps listed in this procedure

This procedure addresses the Platform Operations workflow for safe removal of deconstruction targets within a configuration file. NIE has outlined the overall process in their wiki

  • NIE is responsible for the return of all hardware.
    • NIE, NetSupport or NetStrategy will generate a deconstruction ticket and delegate it to CSD for processing.
    This ticket will be used for the entire deconstruction process. If a deconstruction request is received from outside of the Network departments, direct the requestor to File a Networks CR.
  • CSD will execute all required safety checks then proceed to safely remove all targets according to Akamai internal guidelines.
    Safe Removal requires all respective Akamai software, secrets and access have been removed from the targets. upon completion NIE/requestor is notified, targets can then be physically removed and/or transposed.
  • Upon completion of Safe Removal, SysOps will proceed to update the config files and delegate the ticket
  • For Cancellation and Rescheduling proceed to Cancel a Deconstruction.
